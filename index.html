Meet Your Fitness Trainer, Nicole!

Nicole Tanner is a group fitness instructor and personal trainer located the Greater Philadelphia area. She has been in the industry for 8 years. Nicole grew up dancing and eventually began teaching dance fitness at her local fitness centers, and then expanded her passion for fitness into personal training too.

Developing as fitness professional, she wanted to create experiences that did more than change bodies. She wanted to teach classes that brought smiles to the studio and ease of mind and body outside of the gym too.

Having launched several class formats in the local area, Nicole currently personal trains and teaches classes at night. She loves teaching Barre and BollyX- The Bollywood Workout, where she also gets to share her passion for dance with new instructors as a BollyX Community Mentor.

When Nicole isn't training she is usually playing around with new coffee drinks, hanging out with her cats, or on some sort of adventure out and about- whether it be an art festival or hiking trail.
